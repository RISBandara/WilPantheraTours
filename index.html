Discover Wilpattu With WilPanthera Tours

Wilpattu National Park is the largest and one of the oldest national parks in Sri Lanka, renowned for its leopard population and natural lakes called "villus". At WilPanthera Tours, we offer unforgettable safari experiences with our expert guides who know the park intimately.

Our mission is to provide safe, comfortable, and memorable wildlife experiences while promoting sustainable tourism practices that help conserve this precious ecosystem.
